Privacy Policy

At SinzTools, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to being transparent about how we handle data.

No Image Uploads

Most of our current tools do not require uploading images to any server. All image processing for these tools happens directly in your web browser on your device.

No File Storage

For current tools, we do not store, save, or retain any of your image files. Once you close your browser or navigate away, no traces remain on our servers.

No Personal Data Collection

SinzTools does not collect, track, or store any personal information, IP addresses, or usage data at this stage. We don't use cookies or tracking technologies.

Future Changes

In the future, we may add basic analytics or advertising to support the site, or introduce tools that require server-side processing. Any such changes will be optional, clearly disclosed, and this policy will be updated accordingly.

If you have questions, please contact us.
