How this NEET percentile estimator works

Enter your NEET marks out of 720. Click the "Estimate Percentile" button to get an approximate percentile estimation. The result is for reference only and should not be considered official.

NEET marks and percentile explanation

NEET percentile indicates the percentage of candidates who scored equal to or below your marks. Official percentiles are calculated by NTA using normalization based on exam difficulty across different sessions.
