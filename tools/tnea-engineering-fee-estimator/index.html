Engineering College Fee Estimator (TNEA)

Estimate approximate annual fees for engineering colleges in Tamil Nadu through TNEA counselling. This tool provides ranges based on college type and category.

How engineering college fees work in Tamil Nadu

Engineering college fees in Tamil Nadu vary based on whether the college is government, government-aided, or private. Fees include tuition, exam fees, and other charges. Hostel fees are additional if you choose to stay on campus.

What this fee estimator shows

This tool shows approximate annual fee ranges for reference. It combines tuition and hostel fees based on your selections. Actual fees depend on the specific college and course.

Factors that affect engineering college fees

Fees depend on college type (government, aided, private), student category (OC, BC, MBC, SC, ST), course branch, and whether you need hostel accommodation. Scholarships and concessions may apply for certain categories.

Example fee estimation

For a government college with hostel for an OC student: approximately ₹30,000 – ₹60,000 per year. This is just an example; use the calculator below for your estimate.
