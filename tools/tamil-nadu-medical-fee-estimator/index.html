Medical College Fee Estimator (Tamil Nadu)

Estimate approximate annual fees for medical and allied medical courses in Tamil Nadu. This tool provides ranges based on course and college type.

How medical college fees work in Tamil Nadu

Medical college fees in Tamil Nadu vary based on the course (MBBS, BDS, AYUSH, allied), college type (government, aided, private, deemed), and whether hostel is required. Fees include tuition, exam fees, and other charges.

What this fee estimator shows

This tool shows approximate annual fee ranges for reference. It combines tuition and hostel fees based on your selections. Actual fees depend on the specific college and course.

Factors affecting medical college fees

Fees depend on course type, college category, hostel accommodation, and reservation quotas. Scholarships and concessions may apply for certain categories.

Example fee estimation

For MBBS in a government college with hostel: approximately ₹40,000 – ₹70,000 per year. This is just an example; use the calculator below for your estimate.
