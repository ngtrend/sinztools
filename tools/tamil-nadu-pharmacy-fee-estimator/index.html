Pharmacy College Fee Estimator (Tamil Nadu)

Estimate approximate annual fees for pharmacy courses in Tamil Nadu. This tool provides ranges based on course and college type.

How pharmacy college fees work

Pharmacy college fees in Tamil Nadu vary based on the course (B.Pharm, Pharm.D, D.Pharm) and whether the college is government or private. Hostel fees are additional if accommodation is needed.

What this estimator shows

This tool shows approximate annual fee ranges for reference. It combines tuition and hostel fees based on your selections. Actual fees depend on the specific college and course.

Example fee estimation

For B.Pharm in a government college with hostel: approximately ₹55,000 – ₹1,20,000 per year. This is just an example; use the calculator below for your estimate.
