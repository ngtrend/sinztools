Anna University CGPA to Percentage Calculator

Convert your Anna University CGPA to percentage using the official formula. This tool runs entirely in your browser for maximum privacy and speed.

How to use the calculator

Simply enter your CGPA score in the input field above. The CGPA should be between 0 and 10. Click the "Calculate Percentage" button to get your percentage instantly. The result will be displayed below the button.

Anna University CGPA to Percentage formula

Anna University uses the following official formula to convert CGPA to percentage:

Percentage = (CGPA × 10) − 7.5

This formula is specific to Anna University and may not apply to other universities or institutions.

Example calculation

If your CGPA is 7.81:

Percentage = (7.81 × 10) − 7.5 = 78.1 − 7.5 = 70.6%

So, a CGPA of 7.81 converts to 70.6%.

Notes & disclaimer

CGPA to percentage conversion rules may vary by regulation or academic year. Always verify with official Anna University documents when required for admissions or employment.
