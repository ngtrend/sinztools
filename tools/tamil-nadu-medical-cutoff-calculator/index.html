Tamil Nadu Medical Cut-off Calculator

Calculate your Tamil Nadu medical admission cut-off using Biology, Physics, and Chemistry marks (out of 100). This tool runs entirely in your browser for maximum privacy and speed.

How Tamil Nadu medical cut-off is calculated

The medical cut-off is calculated based on your Class 12 Biology, Physics, and Chemistry marks. Each subject contributes differently to the final cut-off score, which is out of 100.

Medical cut-off formula (current pattern)

The current Tamil Nadu medical cut-off formula is:

Medical Cut-off = (Biology / 2) + (Physics / 4) + (Chemistry / 4)

This gives a cut-off score out of 100, where Biology contributes 50%, Physics 25%, and Chemistry 25%.

Example calculation

If your marks are:

  • Biology: 86/100
  • Physics: 78/100
  • Chemistry: 72/100

Medical Cut-off = (86 / 2) + (78 / 4) + (72 / 4) = 43 + 19.5 + 18 = 80.5 / 100

So, your medical cut-off is 80.5 out of 100.

Courses that use this cut-off

This cut-off is commonly used for admissions to:

  • MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery)
  • BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery)
  • AYUSH courses (Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Siddha, Homeopathy)
  • Allied Medical courses
  • Other Biology-based medical and paramedical admissions

Notes & disclaimer

This calculator assumes individual subject marks are out of 100, as followed in recent Tamil Nadu board examinations.

Cut-off calculation rules may change based on year, counselling authority, or government policy. Always verify with official counselling notifications.
