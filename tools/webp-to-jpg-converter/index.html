WEBP to JPG Converter

Convert your WEBP images to JPG format instantly in your browser. No uploads required - your files stay private and secure.

How WEBP to JPG conversion works

This tool uses your browser's built-in canvas technology to convert WEBP images to JPG format. When you select a WEBP file, it's processed locally without uploading to any server, ensuring fast and secure conversions.

Important: JPG format does not support transparency. Any transparent areas in your WEBP image will become white in the converted JPG.

Why convert WEBP to JPG?

WEBP offers excellent compression but has limited compatibility with older devices and software. Converting to JPG ensures broader compatibility while maintaining good image quality. JPG is widely supported across all platforms and devices.

Privacy & security

All image processing happens locally in your browser. No files are uploaded or stored on any server. Your images remain completely private and secure.
