Tamil Nadu Engineering Cutoff Calculator

Calculate your Tamil Nadu State Board engineering admission cutoff using your Class 12 marks in Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry. This tool runs entirely in your browser for maximum privacy and speed.

How to use the cutoff calculator

Enter your Tamil Nadu State Board Class 12 marks for Mathematics (out of 200), Physics (out of 100), and Chemistry (out of 100). Click the "Calculate Cutoff" button to get your engineering admission cutoff instantly. The result will be displayed below the button.

Tamil Nadu engineering cutoff formula

The Tamil Nadu engineering cutoff is calculated using the following formula:

Cutoff = (Maths / 2) + (Physics / 4) + (Chemistry / 4)

This formula is based on the commonly used Tamil Nadu State Board engineering admission rules.

Example cutoff calculation

If your marks are:

  • Mathematics: 180/200
  • Physics: 85/100
  • Chemistry: 90/100

Cutoff = (180 / 2) + (85 / 4) + (90 / 4) = 90 + 21.25 + 22.5 = 133.75

So, the cutoff is 133.75.

Important notes & disclaimer

This cutoff calculator is based on commonly used Tamil Nadu State Board engineering admission rules. Cutoff calculation methods may change depending on government policies or admission regulations. Always verify with official counselling authorities.
