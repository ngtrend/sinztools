About SinzTools

SinzTools is a collection of free, browser-based image tools designed to help users convert, compress, and optimize images quickly and easily.

Most tools run entirely in your web browser, meaning your images never leave your device. There's no need to upload files to any server for these tools, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Our privacy-first approach means we don't collect, store, or share any personal data or image files at this stage. SinzTools is built for everyday users who need simple, reliable image utilities without complications.

In the future, we may add tools that require limited server-side processing, which will be clearly disclosed and explained.
